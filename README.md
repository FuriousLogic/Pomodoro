# Pomodoro

## Arduino project for simple Pomodoro helper

This will control 4 leds, 1 red, 1 yellow & 2 greens.  Plus a piezo buzzer.
The stares are:
- Live pomodoro (red)
- Pomodoro completed (red & yellow)
- Short break (green)
- Long break (green & green)
- Break over (green & yellow)
- Neutral (yellow)
